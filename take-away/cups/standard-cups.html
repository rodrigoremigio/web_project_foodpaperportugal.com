STANDARD CUPS

At FOOD PAPER we support the use biodegradable and sustainable materials in cups. It becomes evident that the disposable cup has a very short shelf life, following our ecological footprint, it makes no sense to use cups produced from polluting materials or petroleum derivatives.

If our customers need anonymous cups, we offer standard cups in three sustainable materials: paper, kraft paper and CPLA (crystallized poly lactic acid).

At FOOD PAPER the choice of the cup is essential because its versatility is important. Making available to our customers paper cups for cold and hot liquids in the measures 4 Oz (120ml), 7 Oz (210 ml) and 12 Oz (350 ml).
